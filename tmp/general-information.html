Standard Delivery

Delivery times may vary by product. See the product page detail for the items you're interested in for the most up to date estimate. Overall, our standard Estimated Delivery Time is 5-10 Days.

Delivery Outside the Contiguous U.S.

Shipments to Alaska, Guam, Hawaii, Puerto Rico or U.S. Virgin Islands may require additional shipping charges and certain items cannot be shipped to these locations. Please call or email your order and we will quote the shipping cost. Due to air transportation restrictions, our Silk Flower & Plant Cleaner cannot ship outside the continental U.S. Orders outside the Contiguous USA can't be placed on the website.

APO/FPO Addresses

Please note that size restrictions apply to APO/FPO addresses. Some of the larger florals and plants featured on our website, as well those items containing the truck delivery icon, cannot be shipped to these address types. Please submit your order on-line and we will call or email you prior to shipment if these restrictions apply to your order.

Remote Delivery Addresses within the Continental U.S.

Certain remote delivery addresses within the continental U.S. may require additional shipping charges. Please submit your order on-line and we will call or email you prior to shipment if additional charges apply to your order.

International Shipments

Currently Petals.com does not accept international shipments.

Are Petals products real or artificial?

All of our lifelike floral products featured on Petals.com are artificial.

Are Petals Products for Outdoor Use?

No they are not suitable for outdoor use or exposure to sun/heat — Indoor use only.

Are your flowers really silk?

Although artificial flowers and plants are commonly referred to as “silk”, the materials used to design our lifelike silk flower arrangements and artificial plants vary by style and cover a wide range of materials such as various fabrics, synthetic and poly-resin materials.

How are your arrangements and plants packaged?

We pride ourselves on ensuring that your product will arrive in perfect condition ready for immediate enjoyment. We expertly package every item using our proprietary packaging methods to not only prevent damage but also so that you just open the box and place the item in the intended location.

How are your arrangements and plants shipped?

The majority of our items are shipped via premium shipping carriers such as FED EX and UPS.

What is the clear substance in the bottom of the vases?

Many of our silk floral designs are created using our special "crystal clear acrylic water" which creates the illusion of fresh cut stems in water.

Do I need to order a container for my plant or tree?

Unless otherwise indicated, all Petals floor plants and trees arrive potted in a non-decorative liner. Typically the liner is either a plastic or paper-mache container suitable for placement inside your own container. When you purchase a decorative container from Petals.com along with your silk plant or artificial tree, unless specifically stated otherwise, your item will arrive securely potted in the container of your choice and will be topped with our lush green moss.

How do I clean and maintain my Petals arrangements and plants?

Petals plants require very little maintenance. Feather dust periodically as part of normal home or office cleaning and apply Silkflower & Plant Cleaner to floral arrangements on a quarterly basis. We recommend that you retain the original shipping carton to store seasonal arrangements.

Can I send Petals products as gifts?

Why send a gift that only lasts for only a few days when you can send a constant reminder of your love, appreciation and friendship? Let our friendly and knowledgeable staff help you select the perfect item! When sent as a gift, all Petals silk floral designs and plants arrive with a beautiful gift card with your personalized message.

Do you offer “Express Delivery”?

Yes, Express Delivery options (order must be placed by 12 noon ET) are available on all items found in our Express Delivery category. We offer expedited ground, 2-3 business day and Next business day service. If you are in hurry to receive your order containing non-express gift items, we suggest calling our customer service department and we will make every effort to try to expedite your order.
